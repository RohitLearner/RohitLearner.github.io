About me

I'm Rohit Kumar Singh (b. 1996, Odisha). I'm a multidisciplinary freelance web designer from Pune. I build websites, infographics, and digital products. Design to me is a way of life. It's not a work, but a passion that you grow with everyday. I've been a self-taught designer for most of my career and has been dabbling with code and Wordpress from time to time.

I am a modern magician, except I transform complicated technical ideas into user-friendly images before the eyes of the client. I've been fortunate to work with successful entrepreneurs and talented people that have assisted me with my journey of becoming a better designer. I love learning and creating innovative solutions for the digital world, forever striving for flawlessness and the best possible outcome.

I’m constantly ready for new design opportunities and have plenty of room up to my sleeve for a few award-winning performances. You can find me hanging around LinkedIn and Google+.

I design for almost everything.
